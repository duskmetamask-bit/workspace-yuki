# Personal Growth Challenges

Daily challenges drawn from proven self-improvement frameworks.
Rotate daily. Never repeat within 14 days.

---

## HABIT MASTERY (Atomic Habits / James Clear)
1. "Identify your ONE keystone habit today and protect it like your life depends on it."
2. "Design your environment for the behavior you want. Remove one friction point."
3. "Complete your hardest task before 10 AM. No exceptions."
4. "Do one thing today that advances your main goal. Just one. But do it."
5. "End the day with a review: What worked? What didn't? What will you do differently tomorrow?"
6. "Habit stacking: attach a new habit to an existing one. What's your anchor today?"

## DISCIPLINE (David Goggins)
7. "Callus your mind. Do something uncomfortable today and finish it."
8. "No zero days. Whatever you do today, make sure it's not nothing."
9. "Do the thing you don't want to do. First. Without thinking."
10. "You are who you are right now. The only thing separating you from who you want to be is reps."

## PHYSICAL (Huberman / General)
11. "20 minutes of sunlight exposure before 10 AM. Non-negotiable."
12. "Cold shower. 3 minutes. No exceptions."
13. "No phone for the first 90 minutes after waking. Let your brain warm up."
14. "Walk 30 minutes today. Active recovery counts."
15. "Sleep is a weapon. Tonight: no screens 60 min before bed, cool room, same time."

## MONEY MINDSET (Naval / Hormozi)
16. "Make one decision today thatsimplifies your business or life. Complexity is the enemy."
17. "Get in one rep of selling. One outreach, one offer, one conversation."
18. "You learn by shipping. What's one thing you can ship today that moves the needle?"
19. "Stop planning. Start executing. Perfect is the enemy of done."
20. "What's the highest leverage thing you can do today? Do that first."

## CONTENT / BRAND (Builder Mindset)
21. "Post something today that makes you uncomfortable. Vulnerability builds audiences."
22. "Engage with 10 people in your space before you post anything. Give value first."
23. "Document your process. Not the destination. The journey."
24. "Reply to 3 posts with something worth reading. Not generic. Actually valuable."

## MINDSET (Various)
25. "Write down your #1 fear today. Then write why it's probably wrong."
26. "Ask yourself: What would I do if I knew I couldn't fail? Do one piece of it today."
27. "Clear your mind of all noise. 10 minutes. No input. Just you and your thoughts."
28. "You don't need more information. You need more action. Pick the thing you've been putting off. Do it."

---

## Daily Challenge Selection
The briefing picks challenge #X where X = (day of year) % 28 + 1
This gives non-repeating rotation for ~2 weeks.
