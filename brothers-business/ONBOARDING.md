## WHAT YOU NEED TO DO BEFORE THE FIRST JOB

### 1. LEGAL + LICENCES (Week 1)

- [ ] **Register business name** → ASIC.gov.au (search first to check availability)
  - Cost: $33–$82
  - Recommended: "Arctic Air Perth" or "Arctic Air & Refrigeration"
  
- [ ] **Get ABN** → ATO.gov.au (free, instant if eligible)
  
- [ ] **Refrigerant handling licence** → DEMIRS WA
  - Website: commerce.wa.gov.au/demirs
  - Required by law to handle refrigerants
  - Cost: ~$200–$400 depending on licence type
  
- [ ] **Electrical licence** (if doing sparky work)
  - If only doing refrigeration: may not need separate electrical licence
  - For split system installation: typically need a licensed electrician for the electrical connection
  - Check: energysafety.wa.gov.au

- [ ] **Public liability insurance** → BizCover.com.au
  - Minimum $5M coverage
  - Cost: ~$1,500–$3,000/year
  - Get 3 quotes minimum

- [ ] **Open business bank account**
  - Most banks: Bank of Queensland, Bendigo Bank, Westpac
  - Take ABN + business name registration + ID
