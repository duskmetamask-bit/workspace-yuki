## WHAT TO SAY ON THE PHONE

**Opening line:** "Arctic Air Perth, [Name] speaking."

**When they call:**
1. "What's the issue you're experiencing?"
2. "Is it cooling, not heating, making noise, or something else?"
3. "Do you know the brand and roughly how old it is?"
4. "Okay, based on what you've described, this sounds like [common issue]. We can have someone out [today/tomorrow]. What's your address?"

**Quote format:** "For a service call like that, we charge [range] depending on what we find. Call-out is [fee]. If it's a simple fix, you're looking at [lower end]. If it needs parts, we talk before we proceed."

**Urgency close:** "We're booking out for [next few days]. If it's urgent, I can get someone out this afternoon if you call before [time]."
