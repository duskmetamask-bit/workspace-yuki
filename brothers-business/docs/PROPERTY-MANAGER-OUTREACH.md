# PROPERTY MANAGER OUTREACH — Arctic Air Perth

## Best Perth Property Managers (Northern Suburbs)

Research and target these areas first:
- Joondalup: Largest suburban centre, many rental properties
- Wanneroo: High density rental area
- Stirling: Mix of residential + commercial
- Greenwood/Scarborough: Mid-range rentals
- Duncraig/Kingsley: Family rentals

### Types to Target
- Property management companies with 50+ properties
- Independent property managers (sole operators)
- Real estate offices with in-house property management
- Student accommodation providers (Curtin, ECU areas)

### Script — Cold Call

**Opening:** "Hi [Name], I'm [Name] from Arctic Air Perth — we're a licensed aircon and refrigeration business based in Joondalup. I wanted to introduce ourselves — do you have 2 minutes?"

**Pitch:** "We're targeting property managers in the northern suburbs who need a reliable contractor. We're licensed, insured, and we're building preferred contractor relationships. Do you currently have a preferred aircon contractor?"

**If yes:** "How are they going? Any frustrations? If you're open to it, I'd love to be a backup option for when they can't get there."

**If no:** "Perfect — we're new but established. Would it be worth sending over our details and a couple of references?"

**Close:** "Can I send you an email with our details and pricing? And would you be open to a 10-minute call next week?"

## PROPERTY MANAGER OBJECTION HANDLING

**"We already have someone"**
"That's completely fine. We're not here to push anyone out. The value we'd add is urgent call-outs and transparent pricing. Would you be open to having us as a backup for when [current contractor] is booked out?"

**"I don't have time to change"**
"No stress at all. Can I send you an email with our details? If anything changes or you're frustrated with the current setup, we're here."

**"You're more expensive"**
"Can you tell me what you're currently paying? Our rates are transparent — I can tell you exactly what you'd pay before any work starts. That's how we operate."
