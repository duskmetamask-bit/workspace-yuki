---

## RULES FOR DUSK

1. **Reply within 2 hours** — leads go cold fast
2. **Be human** — this is a conversation, not a script
3. **Don't oversell** — answer the question, offer the call
4. **If unsure — say so** — "I'm not sure if we'd be the right fit, but here's what I'd look for..."
5. **Book the call yourself** — send Calendly link only after positive reply
6. **Track every reply** — mark in lead-crm.md what they said and what you sent

---

## OUTBOUND SCRIPT (for booked call — 30 min)

**First 5 min:** Build rapport, understand where they are, what's been tried
- "Tell me about [Company]. What does your week look like?"
- "What's the one thing that takes the most time that you wish just... worked?"
- "Have you tried any automation tools before? What happened?"

**Next 15 min:** Deep dive on their workflows
- Map out the current process
- Find the bottlenecks
- Identify what breaks when it goes wrong

**Next 5 min:** Diagnose and position
- "Here's what I'm seeing — [their biggest opportunity]"
- "Most companies at your stage try to [common mistake]"
- "The way I'd approach it is [outline the fix]"

**Last 5 min:** Close or defer
- "Does that make sense as a starting point?"
- "If you wanted to go deeper, here's what the full engagement looks like"
- "Not ready to commit today? Here's what I'd recommend you do first"
